                           About me

Well here we are. Little ol' me. My name is Michelle Counts, although many people know me as Kiki online.
I am a 29 year old transwoman*, a student at UMBC, an avid gamer, a massive nerd, ex-NASA employee, and a wanna-be programmer.
I've been in a relationship with the same woman for the last 10 years, although we aren't married yet, we are working on that.
I'm left-handed, have one younger sister, I love all kinds of animals but especially cats and dogs.
I'm not much for sports these days, but when I was younger I played defense in lacrosse and practiced HEMA.
I actually had to give up both due to some bad injuries to my ankles from playing goalie. That ball is basically a rock covered in rubber at 90mph.
I spend most of my free time playing video games, playing TTRPGS and listening to music.
I've said before that of my two favorite characters, Kiki and Val, that Kiki is who I wish I was and Val is who I am.
In a lot of ways this is true, but there's obviously a lot of differences. Despite my wishes, I am not in fact magic.
Now, I'm not going to talk too much about Kiki. I'm going to show pictures of her, but that's about it.
Val gets her own about page, and that should speak a lot about me as well as her.
